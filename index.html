About Me

I am a dedicated and experienced teacher from London with over 6 years of experience in both tutoring and classroom teaching. I currently teach a Year 3 class and have extensive experience working with children across different educational settings, including private, public, mainstream, and SEN schools.

I’ve been consistently praised for my patient and approachable teaching style, which helps make learning an enjoyable experience for my pupils. I work with children from Pre-Reception age up to Year 9, tailoring my lessons to meet each child’s unique needs.

My approach is hands-on, and I am deeply committed to helping children grow and succeed academically and personally. In addition to traditional subjects, I focus on enhancing students' communication, confidence, creativity, attention span, and problem-solving skills.

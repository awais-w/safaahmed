About Me

I am a dedicated and experienced teacher from London with over 6 years of experience in both tuition and classroom teaching. I currently teach a Year 3 class and have extensive experience working with children across different educational settings, including private, public, mainstream, and SEN schools. I hold a valid DBS Enhanced Check, ensuring full safeguarding compliance.

I've been consistently praised for my patient and approachable teaching style, which helps make learning an enjoyable experience for my pupils. I work with children from Pre-Reception age up to Year 9, tailoring my tuition services to meet each child's unique needs.

My approach is hands-on, and I am deeply committed to helping children grow and succeed academically and personally. In addition to traditional subjects, I focus on enhancing pupils' communication, confidence, creativity, attention span, and problem-solving skills. Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to typical questions parents ask about my private tuition services in London.

What age groups and academic levels do you tutor?

I tutor children from Pre-Reception (ages 3–4) up to Year 9 (ages 13–14).

Every session is customised to support the child's academic development, whether they are building early phonics skills, strengthening their primary mathematics foundations, preparing for selective exams, or studying key secondary-school humanities. See the full list of subjects I cover.

Where do the tuition sessions take place?

I offer flexible delivery options depending on your location and convenience:

  • In-Person Tuition: Conducted in the comfort of your home across West and Central London (covering Ealing, Kensington, Chelsea, and surrounding areas).
  • Online Tuition: Interactive and engaging remote sessions conducted via private video calling, using collaborative whiteboards and digital learning games.
How do you prepare pupils for the 11+ entrance exams?

My 11+ preparation programme is highly structured and results-driven:

We work systematically on verbal and non-verbal reasoning, core Maths and English curriculum extension, creative writing, and test time management. Preparation is aligned with the standards set by the National Curriculum. By using target-driven assessment mocks and building problem-solving resilience, I help guide pupils smoothly into their preferred grammar and independent schools.

What materials and resources are included in the hourly rate?

The hourly rate of £45/hr is fully inclusive of all premium education resources:

I provide tailored physical books, fully personalised learning worksheets, high-quality reference books, and full access to premium educational learning games. There are no additional charges for curriculum materials or planning.
